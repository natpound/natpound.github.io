  • About

    Natalie is a music director, pianist, orchestrator, and rep coach.

    She is a multi-instrumentalist spanning over all orchestral families, benefitting her orchestrations with a deep understanding of each instrument.

    She is currently completing an MA in Music Direction for MT at the Royal Academy of Music, and has a masters degree in Physics from University College London.

    Credits

    Credits as MD include:

    The Musical of Musicals (The Musical!) (Bridewell Theatre), Rapunzel (Bedford Corn Exchange), Songs For A New World (Drayton Arms Theatre), Hall of Mirrors (Network Theatre, London), From Here To Eternity (also orchestrator of 2018 revised production, Bloomsbury Theatre), Ordinary Days (Camden People's Theatre), RENT (RADA Studios), Elegies for Angels, Punks, and Raging Queens (Charity benefit), Women on the Verge of a Nervous Breakdown (Bloomsbury Studio), Antigone (Shaw Theatre), Murder Ballad, Disney's High School Musical, We Will Rock You, The Rocky Horror Show (University College London)

    Credits as AMD include:

    Chicago (The Place Theatre Bedford), Paperboy (British Youth Musical Theatre, Lyric Theatre Belfast), Into the Woods (Auditorium Intercomunale Primiero), Sister Act (Auditorium Intercomunale Primiero)
