  • About

      Natalie is a music director, pianist, & orchestrator, and is currently Associate MD on SIX (West End).
      She is a multi-instrumentalist spanning over all orchestral families, benefitting her orchestrations, and well versed in music technology.

        She's passionate about developing new musical theatre work, and has also showcased a composition of her own at the Garrick theatre as part of The Chamber Musical Sessions.

          She graduated with Distinction from the MA Music Direction for MT at the Royal Academy of Music, and also has a masters degree in Physics from University College London.
