    MUSICAL DIRECTOR | ORCHESTRATOR [EB-image]


    Nat is a London based musical director and orchestrator who studied at the Royal Academy of Music. Often found working on new musicals, she has worked at venues such as the Donmar Warehouse, the National Theatre, and the Almeida Theatre. Recent credits include the world premiere of Roald Dahl's The Witches, and the UK premiere of Next To Normal which is set to transfer to the West End in summer. She also has a masters degree in Physics from University College London.

    Somewhere in the misty midst of lockdown she decided to pick up enough HTML/CSS coding skills to make this website herself, but not enough to actually make it work well. Enjoy the rough edges.
